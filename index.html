Logotype

Print 4 Mo Digital display 390 Ko Logotype 5,5 Mo

The Betclic logo is simple and easy to use.
Thanks to its rectangle, it is protected for all uses and our red is always visible. It is always written in white on a Red background.

Colours

Betclic is always written in white on a Red Magma background. When the logo is applied to a color or an image whose color is indistinguishable from Magma Red, it is then exceptionally recommended to remove the logotype for a more qualitative result. As soon as the frame is visible, it must be kept.

Monochromes

In rare conditions, and mainly in merchandising, technical constraints do not allow the use of several colors.
It is in these cases and only in these cases that the monochrome versions of our logotype must be used.

Frame

The Betclic red rectangle is an integral part of the logotype. It maintains the brand's red presence and ensures legibility.

The rectangle's dimensions are based on a square that matches the height of the interior of the 'E'. This square is then aligned in width with the edges of the 'B' and 'C', and in height with the 'L'.

Binding formats

For extra thin and extra wide supports, you still need to respect the frame as the protection area. If the logotype is close to touching one or more sides of the support, it's preferable to let it touch by either increasing the size or slightly adjusting its position.

Don'ts

The logotype, featuring white text on a red rectangle, is a critical element of our brand identity. To maintain the integrity and consistency of our visual representation, it is strictly prohibited to alter the logotype in any of the following ways:

  1. Deformation
  2. Color Changes
  3. New design
  4. Rotation or Flipping
  5. Frame transparency
  6. Frame Removal
  7. Frame modification
  8. Any other modification…

For any questions or clarifications regarding the proper use of the logotype, please contact the brand management team.

Quality

In order to ensure optimal display, we recommend using the vector version of the logotype in all cases where it is possible.

Indeed, if the pixelated formats used in the native size offer a qualitative rendering and that certain formats such as PNG and PSD also offer transparency. Vector formats are the only ones to offer a perfect rendering regardless of the display size in addition to the systematic respect of transparencies.

Sponsoring

For visibility purposes in sponsorship communications, a specific version of the logo with a reduced protection area has been created. This version of the logotype should be used only in exceptional cases and requires prior internal approval.

Références

Adapting colors based on the medium is essential to maintain consistency and visual impact across all platforms. Please ensure that the specified color references are accurately applied and adjusted according to the medium.

For print, use CMYK color codes, ensuring color fidelity in physical reproduction.

For digital displays (web, apps, etc.), use RGB or HEX codes, optimized for accurate on-screen display.

Typography

Betclic Font Family 7 Mo

Corporate

First of all, it is recommended to use the Betclic Font as a priority, rather than its Condensed and Expanded versions. In addition, Condensend should generally be used for narrow displays and Italics reserved to highlight actions or information relating to our offering.

Accompanying

Apart from titles and certain important messages using Betclic typography, we favor simple typographies in our products. Apart from the Apple ecosystem, the typography used is Google’s Open Font Roboto.
